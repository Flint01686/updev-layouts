About

Every day, travelers need unusual equipment - they go to Ridestore for it. Founded in Sweden more than 15 years ago (since 2006), Ridestore is known for its impressive selection of brands of outdoor goods. Focusing on customers at the heart of its operational strategy, the company offers regional online stores in Sweden, Finland, Norway, Denmark, Poland, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Italy, France, Spain, the Netherlands and the United Kingdom.

We have developed the virtual fitting room for Ridestore.

Challenge

  • We gathered the target audience through Instagram API: recognized sports brands in users' photos (such as Adidas, Nike, Puma, Dope, and others)
  • Optimized the page loading speed from 0.45 sec to 0.25 sec
  • We fought for SEO positions in Google search results. As a result, Ridestore is at the top in different countries of the world and overtook the Dope brand website for "Buy Dope" request
  • Every two weeks, we tracked the queries that took the most time and optimized the app, did code review and refactoring
  • The servers were at the limit, but they survived thanks to regular optimization and use PReact.js – a fast 3kB alternative to React

What was at the Start?

Linus (Founder) already had a fully functioning online store on the market, which brought income to his company, and his own clothing brand Dope.

Linus wanted to increase the turnover and attract more potential buyers. He had the idea of implementing functionality that none of his competitors had provided at that time: a virtual fitting room, where you can put any clothes from the provided assortment on a virtual mannequin.

He also came up with a brilliant idea for marketing promotion: using AI to analyze user photos in social networks, identify buyers of sports brands (Adidas, Nike, Puma, Reebok, and others) and launch targeted advertising on them.

Provided Services

  • AI Image Recognition
  • Development and Testing
  • Code Review and Refactoring
  • Code Instructions (for example, run locally, publish to the server)

Cooperation

Duration: 1 year by T&M

To implement the founder's idea, we have developed two projects: a virtual fitting room and a script for analyzing the target audience from Instagram.

The main challenge was to develop a free-bug app, taking into account the nuances of mobile social network browsers, and quickly loading to withstand a high load of servers. During the year, we have done a tremendous job of speeding and freeing the project from bugs.

Technologies

Front-end

  • PReact.js
  • Sass
  • Webpack
  • Karma (unit-tests)

Back-end

  • Django REST Framework (Python)
  • PostgreSQ

Marketing Tool:

  • Vision AI
  • Instagram API

Results

In close cooperation between the marketing team and the dev team, we increased the visits to 10-12 thousand per day and the company's revenue tripled
