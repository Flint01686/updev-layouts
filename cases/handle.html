About

Handle (ex. Apartment Ocean) offers an effective marketing tool – a conversational assistant (chatbot, website widget) based on artificial intelligence for the real estate management sector. Every property management company and property owner can benefit from automating their customer service tasks. It is used to increase customer conversions on websites and to reduce agent costs.

Challenge

  • We gathered the target audience through Instagram API: recognized sports brands in users' photos (such as Adidas, Nike, Puma, Dope, and others)
  • Optimized the page loading speed from 0.45 sec to 0.25 sec
  • We fought for SEO positions in Google search results. As a result, Ridestore is at the top in different countries of the world and overtook the Dope brand website for "Buy Dope" request
  • Every two weeks, we tracked the queries that took the most time and optimized the app, did code review and refactoring
  • The servers were at the limit, but they survived thanks to regular optimization and use PReact.js – a fast 3kB alternative to React

What was at the Start?

Junjie and his partner Nick (CEO) had the idea and the demo version of the product at the start, thanks to which they attracted the first round of investments in the amount of $200,000in September 2016.

Their goal was to attract a new round of investments, which they will direct to scaling (marketing and further development of the product), after beta testing and entering the market.

To enter the market, it was necessary to develop a full-fledged working minimal version of the product (MVP), which includes a chatbot based on artificial intelligence, an administrative panel for the possibility of creating and managing a chatbot with a personal account for each client, and a company presentation website for marketing.

Cooperation

Duration: 1 year by T&M

To implement the founder's idea, we have developed two projects: a virtual fitting room and a script for analyzing the target audience from Instagram.

The main challenge was to develop a free-bug app, taking into account the nuances of mobile social network browsers, and quickly loading to withstand a high load of servers. During the year, we have done a tremendous job of speeding and freeing the project from bugs.

Functional

The product is designed specifically for long-term and short-term rental of apartments.

  • Immediately provides suitable options for apartments to meet the specified requirements of the user. For example, a selection of apartments near the beach, provide a choice for a certain number of tenants.
  • Helps the user select an available time and schedule a visit to the apartment, then sends a notification to the agent.
  • Directs the user to view the apartment remotely if the virtual tour is available
  • Answer questions from the user. For example, prompts the user for check-in and check-out times.
  • Tells whether the client can bring their pets with them.
  • Help to book and cancel reservations.

Results

During the work process, the first real estate management companies were connected, beta testing was conducted, and the founders received their first reviews. After improving the product based on reviews, they successfully entered the market.

As a result of confirmation of the business model, the second round of investments for $300,000 was attracted.
